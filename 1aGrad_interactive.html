$\nabla f(\textbf{r}) $

$\nabla f(\textbf{r})$, gives the gradient at any point on the scalar field $f(\textbf{r})$. Note that $\nabla f$ is a vector field, as the direction of the vector at any point gives the direction of steepest change.
